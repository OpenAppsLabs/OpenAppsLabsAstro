Open Apps Labs / 5G

Privacy Policy

Effective: January 8, 2026
TL;DR: No data collection. No ads. No tracking. Works offline.

01. Data Collection

I, the developer behind Open Apps Labs, built the "5G" app as a free utility. I do not collect, transmit, or store any Personal Identifiable Information (PII). There are no third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) tracking you.

02. Permissions

The app requests these permissions to display data locally:

  • READ_PHONE_STATE To display network type (LTE/NR) and signal strength.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE To verify active connection status.

03. Third-Party Services

There are no advertisements and no tracking SDKs. The app does not communicate with external servers.

04. Security

Since no data is collected, there is no data to steal. Your settings stay on your device using Android's internal storage.
