Privacy Policy

Effective: January 8, 2026
TL;DR: Coffee does not collect data. It only uses system permissions to keep your screen awake when toggled.

01. Permissions

This app interacts with Android System settings solely to prevent the screen from sleeping.

  • WAKE_LOCK Allows the app to signal the OS to keep the screen active.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE Required to show the active notification while the timer is running.

02. Data Collection

I do not track when you turn the screen on or off. The app operates 100% locally on your device.
